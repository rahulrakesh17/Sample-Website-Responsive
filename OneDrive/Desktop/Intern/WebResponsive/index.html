QUALITY AND HONESTY

We work tirelessly in the background to ensure our drinks are made with utmost quality and ethos. At COCOWORKS, all the practices right from procurement, production and dispatch are done in the most ethical and fair means.

PRODUCTION PROCESS

All the coconuts, fruits and vegetables undergo a three-step cleaning process before they’re juiced. Once cleaned our produce is pesticide free by 99.99%. Any external pesticide, dust, wax or bacteria has no place in the COCOWORKS kitchens!
